Festival Guide:
[ Festival of Darkness - Requirements for Participation ]
1. In order to participate in the Festival of Darkness, you must belong to either the Lords of Dawn or the Revolutionaries of Dusk.
2. You may participate at any time during the competition period of the Seven Signs, and no limit is imposed on the number of times you may participate.
3. To request participation, five or more players belonging to the same group of fighters must form a party.
4. The party members' levels must meet the criteria established by the festival. The following is the list of grades used in the Festival of Darkness:
- Less than Level 32 Grade
- Less than Level 43 Grade
- Less than Level 54 Grade
- Less than Level 65 Grade
- No Level Limit Grade

5. All party members' grade must be equal.
