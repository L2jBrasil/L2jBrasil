[ Seal of Strife ]
The Seal of Strife bestows the following abilities:
Weak Defense Mercenaries:
The lord of each castle is only allowed to hire the raw recruits that are weaker than seasoned mercenaries.
Decreased Strength of Castle Gates and Walls:
The strength of castle gates and walls decreases significantly.
Increase in the Cost to Upgrade the Castle Gates and Walls:
Upgrading castle gates and walls becomes very expensive.
Reduced Tax Rate Limit:
The maximum tax rate is reduced.
