Seer Tanapi:
Some time ago, the goblin grave robbers destroyed our ancestors' tomb and ran away with the Scepter of Tantos.
As you know, the Tantos tribe was executed for high treason 100 years ago. But later on, it turned out that it was a misunderstanding caused by the wicked Dwarves' mischief and the Flame Lord mourned bitterly his mistake. Every year we hold a memorial service to seek forgiveness from our falsely charged brothers of Tantos and comfort their souls.
If you recover the Scepter of Tantos, I am sure the Monarch will be very pleased. And the Mark of Glory will be yours for sure!
"I shall bring back the Scepter of Tantos!"
